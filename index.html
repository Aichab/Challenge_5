Audio Theme Framework Demo

The AudioTheme Framework plugin is built to work with any WordPress theme, providing the features needed for a music-oriented website and allowing you to take your content with you anywhere you go — even when switching themes.

TIn this demo, we’ve showcased how the AudioTheme Framework will look with the Twenty Fourteen theme and a little help with this child theme we’ve created. Take a look around at the functionality for displaying gigs and venues, discography (liner notes for the web), and videos.
